Privacy Policy — Many Moons

Last updated: 25 June 2026

Many Moons is a moon-phase companion app. We designed it to be private by default. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with information.

The short version

Many Moons does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There is no account, no login, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking. Everything the app remembers stays on your device.

Information stored on your device

The app saves a small amount of data locally on your device only, so your experience persists between sessions:

  • The moons you have "pressed" (each with its date and phase) and your collection progress.
  • An optional birth date you choose to enter, used only to show you that night's moon.
  • Your settings (theme, and whether the nightly reminder is on).

This information never leaves your device. It is not sent to us or to anyone else. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, it is gone. You can export a backup file yourself from within the app at any time; that file is created on your device and shared only if you choose to share it.

Notifications

If you turn on the optional nightly reminder, the app schedules local notifications on your device to remind you to look at the night's moon. These are generated on the device and require no network connection. You can turn them off any time in the app's settings, or in your device's notification settings.

Permissions

  • Notifications — only if you enable the nightly reminder.
  • Internet — used only to load typefaces; the moon imagery and all features work offline. No personal data is transmitted.

Sharing

When you tap "share" on a moon, the app creates an image on your device and hands it to your device's own share sheet. You choose where (if anywhere) it goes. The app does not upload it.

Children

Many Moons does not collect data from anyone, including children, and contains no ads or in-app purchases.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
